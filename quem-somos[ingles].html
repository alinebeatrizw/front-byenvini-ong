    Who we are?


    BYENVINI ONG is an association that was formed in order to elaborate and observe the life situation of Haitian immigrants in Brazil, specifically in Curitiba. We want to help improve the lives of immigrants Haitians developing projects, and everything that is part of society, as such: employment, family, education and leisure.


    MISSION


    Our mission is to help Haitian immigrants who are in vulnerable situations


    EYESIGHT

    We want to be one of the largest humanitarian resources in the world in the development of Haitian immigrants and we intend to build a school and an orphanage for children in Haiti.




    VALUES


    Dignity, difference, respect, asset and well-being.
