    Why hire a refugee or immigrant ??


    For a refugee to rebuild his life in Brazil, working is essential. Therefore, refugees demonstrate a high degree of motivation, effort and willingness to learn. It is common to find among them people who speak more than one language and have valuable professional qualifications in the national community. Most of the time, they are ready to start the new job immediately and already have complete documentation for the job. Its presence culturally enriches the work environment and the hiring demonstrates the company's social responsibility.
